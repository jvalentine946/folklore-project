The HellHound

The Hellhounds is a supernatural dog, found in folklore. A wide variety of ominous and hell supernatural dogs occur in mythologies around the world, similar to the oft-seen dragon. Features that have been attributed to hellhounds include black fur, glowing red, or, sometimes, yellow eyes, super strength or speed, ghostly or phantom characteristics, foul odor, and sometimes even the ability to talk. It is also said that hellhound's are sent from Hell to kill those who's contracts: sold their souls, have expired, the only one who can see a hellhound, is the one being hunted by it. While it is completely invisible to those it's not after.
