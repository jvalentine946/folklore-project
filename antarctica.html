The Ningen

[Creature Illustration]

Ningens are humanoid creatures that live in the icy waters near Antarctica. Ningens are humanlike and are completely white in color. Some people think they are an undiscovered species of apes who are able to breathe underwater. Sometimes Ningens appear to have a mermaid tale or other fins on their bodies, while others report they have hind legs that enable them to walk on land.

Country of Origin
The tale of the Ningen first surfaced in the early 2000s, emerging from the digital depths of 2channel, a popular Japanese online forum.
The Ningen shares similarities with other cryptids, such as the Loch Ness Monster or mermaids, due to its aquatic nature and humanoid features. However, its unique Antarctic habitat sets it apart, making it a distinct figure within cryptozoology.
