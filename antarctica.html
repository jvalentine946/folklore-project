The Ningen

Ningens are humanoid creatures that live in the icy waters near Antarctica. Ningens are human-like, completely white in color, and span 20-30 meters in length with a mermaid-like tail. Some people think they are an undiscovered species of apes who are able to breathe underwater...

Country of Origin
Creatures Origins:

The creature was first sighted in 1960, along the side of a whaling ship. At first glace, it was thought to be a submarine, but at closer expection, it was living and the beast quickly disappeared without a trace.

Cousins of Another Cryptid:

Thought to be 20-30 meters in length, some witnesses state that the creature is a small, bipedal humanoid, a cousin of the Fresno Nightcrawlers, whom appear to be relatively short creatures (approx. 1.5 meters) with most of their height being made up of their legs as they possess an extremely small upper body.

Explainations:
  • Misidentified whales, sharks, and squids
  • A misidentified iceberg
  • An undiscovered subspecies of beluga
  • Photo manipulation
